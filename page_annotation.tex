\textbf{Annotation}
\end{center}

Inspired by the rise of blockchain and cryptocurrency technologies, this thesis focuses on developing an educational blockchain cryptocurrency prototype that bridges the gap between theoretical study and practical application. This thesis provides an overview of popular blockchain protocols and conducts a technical analysis of consensus algorithms. As a practical component, a modular blockchain system was created using Python, accompanied by a Vue.js blockchain explorer for real-time visualization. To demonstrate the system's extensibility for educational purposes, the prototype was adapted into a modular design featuring multiple use cases for the educational sector. The practical component of the thesis aims toward allowing the students and teachers to explore how blockchain systems work, making complex distributed ledger concepts accessible through a structured approach.
